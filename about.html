Aiswarya Manoj

Playback Singer

Aiswarya profile

Famous as a playback singer, Providing online music classes in both Carnatic and Hindustani. Acquired Sangeeth visharad which is a Seven year degree course in hindustani vocal music from Akhil bharatiya gandharva mahavidyalaya Mumbai. Calicut University first rank holder in Masters degree in Carnatic vocal from Chembai Memorial govt.Music college palakkad.

Sang in more than 1000 albums which includes Hindu Christian Muslim devotionals, Hindi song albums, Tamil, Telugu albums, Malayalam light music etc.

Started music career at the early age of 9 in 1st album “Ramzan Nilavu “a duet with Ganagandharvan Dr.KJ Yesudas. "Chirithooki kaliyadi vava kanna” Guruvayoorappan devotional songs is one of the most famous albums.

Sang duets with famous Playback singers MG Sreekumar, Madhubalakrishnan, Ganesh Sundaram, P.Jayachandran, Sathish Babu, Kannur Sherif, Chengannoor Srikumar, Sudeep kumar, Najim Arshad etc.

Disciple of Pt Ramesh Narayan, Sri Vijay Sursen, Sri Mohan Kumar in Hindustani vocal, Disciple of Sri KVS Babu, Late Sri Nedumangad Sasidharan Nair in Carnatic music.

Done music programs in most of the channels like Asianet, Mathrubhumi, Media one, Amrita TV, Kairali Tv, Darshana Tv, DD Malayalam, Jeevan TV, Kerala Vision etc.
