Music

Music is an extension of emotions through harmonic frequencies. Music stimulates the brain development and relieve stress. Music leads us to positive thinking and develop sense of belonging and connection. It improves the lung capacity and is the best language to communicate feelings. Music is the best therapy for depression and builds confidence. Learning classical music develop higher IQ in teenagers. Diksha music academy provides Vocal music training in Hindustani classical music and Carnatic classical music online for all age groups online.

Hindustani Vocal

Diksha Music Academy offers a golden opportunity to learn pure Hindustani classical music for all ages. Hindustani classical music places more emphasis on improvisation and exploring all aspects of a raga. There are different kharanas in Hindustani classical music. Diksha music academy provides pure style of Hindustani classical music traning from best gurus online for people from all over the world.
