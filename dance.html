What is it?

Dance is An artistic yoga that involves movement of body parts in every elegant manner. Diksha Music Academy provides professional training in Bharatanatyam, Mohiniyattam and kuchipudi. Regular dance practice helps to improve flexibility, balance and increases stamina. Diksha Music Academy offers opportunities for people of all ages to learn dance from any part of the world online. To acquire skill in your favorite art through dance steps and reduce stress, we offer best teachers for you.
